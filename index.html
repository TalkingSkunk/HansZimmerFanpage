Who is Hans Zimmer?

The baby Hans Zimmer started his journey of becoming a world-class film score composer in 1957 in West Germany.
During his tender years of growing up, Hans was deeply influenced by a renown Italian composer Ennio Morricone.

He is responsible for some of the most memorable and iconic music ever created in the recent memory.
